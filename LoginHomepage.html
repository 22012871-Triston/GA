What is a payment gateway?
A payment gateway is a secure online service that enables customers to make payments to your business using various payment methods. It acts as a bridge between your online store and the financial institutions involved in the transaction process . Payment gateways are widely used in e-commerce to streamline and facilitate the payment process for both merchants and customers.

Why do we need a payment gateway?
For any online business, having a payment gateway is crucial. It simplifies, accelerates, and ensures the security of the purchasing process for your customers. Without a payment gateway, you would have to rely on traditional methods such as collecting checks from the post office, which can be time-consuming and less secure . Additionally, a multi-currency payment gateway, like the one offered by our company, Paypex Payments, allows businesses to transact with customers from different countries without compromising a significant portion of their profit due to costly foreign exchange and transfer fees . This feature expands your market reach and provides a seamless payment experience for your international customers.
